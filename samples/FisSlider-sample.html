Fidelity Bank of Texas is proud to be a locally owned and independent bank that’s been part of the Waco community for more than 50 years. We believe in building relationships, not just accounts—and in making banking simple, secure, and centered around you. Whether you’re managing your first checking account, expanding your business, or saving for the future, we’re here to help.
