About Us

Since opening our doors in 1974, Fidelity Bank of Texas has remained locally owned, deeply rooted in Waco, and committed to serving Central Texans with a personal touch. As a true community bank, we make decisions right here at home—and put people before policies.

 

  • Our Story: From one branch to two thriving locations, our commitment has never changed: help our neighbors succeed.
  • Founding in 1973 in Waco
  • Values: independence, community, personal service
  • Ongoing investment in Central Texas growth
  • Community partnerships and sponsorships
